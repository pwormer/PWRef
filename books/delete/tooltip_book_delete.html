First a list is built of references that may be deleted. The list is constructed according to the input search parameters on this page. As a second step the actual references that are to be deleted must be checkmarked.
  • All input fields are optional. The default time interval is from last year to the present year.
  • All input fields (except the years) may contain a substring of the property searched for.
  • If the default time interval is the only input, all book-type publications of this and last year will be returned.
  • Because on input the the default year is 1 CE, publications with that year may be present in the database and should be interpreted as "undated".
  • The specified author may be any of the coauthors of the work searched for, he/she is not necessarily the first author.
  • The specified first and/or last name may be of a (co)editor of proceedings, books, or similar publications.
  • The title field refers to a primary title (title of book or contribution etc.) and may be a substring.
  • The key field is required in BibTex and gives a unique identification of the work.
  • The type of work (book, PhD thesis, etc.) may be "All types", in which case all book-type publications are searched for.
